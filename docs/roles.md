# PropOS Roles

## ADMIN

Full platform access. Can manage users, tenants, billing, and all
operational data. Has unrestricted read and write permissions across
every feature module.

- User management (create, update, deactivate)
- Tenant configuration and settings
- All property, contact, project, interaction, and document operations
- System-wide reporting and analytics

## AGENT

Primary operational role for real estate agents. Manages properties,
contacts, and interactions within their assigned tenant.

- Create, read, update properties
- Manage contacts and buyer relationships
- Log and view interactions
- Upload and access documents
- View assigned projects

## LANDOWNER

Property owners with read access to their own properties and
related documents. Limited write access.

- View owned properties and their status
- Access documents related to their properties
- View interactions tied to their properties
- Update personal contact information

## BUYER

Prospective buyers with access to listed properties and their
own interaction history.

- Browse available properties
- View property details and documents marked as public
- Access personal interaction history
- Update personal contact information

## CONTENT

Content management role for marketing and communications team
members. Manages property listings, descriptions, and media.

- Create and edit property descriptions and media
- Upload and manage marketing documents
- View properties and their current status
- Manage public-facing content and listings
